general rules

  1. One book per month.
  2. For choosing genres:
  1. All members give options and a poll is held to decide on one. Afterwards, each person gives 2-3 book options and another poll is held to decide the book.
  2. If it's someone's birthday month, they choose a genre and provide multiple book options for other members to vote in the poll.
  3. Be respectful about ideas and recommendations.
  4. Meetings are through videocall, but if agreed upon they can be in person
  5. During meetings, make sure everyone finishes speaking before commenting.
  6. Comments and reactions are welcomed in the groupchat but make sure to not include anything considered a "spoiler" as to not ruin the other's reading.
